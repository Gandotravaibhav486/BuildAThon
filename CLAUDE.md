# CLAUDE.md — LexClear Project Context

## Project Overview

LexClear is a Legal Contract Analyser designed to help everyday Indians understand the contracts they sign. The product bridges the gap between scanning a document and truly comprehending its implications.

## Problem Statement

- 4 out of 5 Indians sign contracts without reading them
- 1 in 3 tenants face deposit disputes due to misunderstood clauses
- Legal language is dense, long, and often multilingual in the Indian context

## Target User

People who regularly encounter legal contracts but lack the time or expertise to read them thoroughly — specifically:
- Employees joining a new company (employment agreements, NDAs)
- Individuals moving into a rented flat (rental/lease agreements)

## Core Features

1. **Risk Categorisation** — Classify every clause as High, Medium, or Low Risk
2. **Chatbot** — Allow users to ask follow-up questions and drill into specific clauses
3. **Hidden Key References / Keywords** — Surface buried obligations, deadlines, and penalties
4. **Long Document Support** — Process documents of 10 or more pages without losing context
5. **Multilingual** — Support Indian regional languages alongside English

## Development Guidelines

- No code has been written yet; this is the planning and context-setting phase
- Prioritise the Indian legal and linguistic context in all design decisions
- Features should be usable by non-lawyers — avoid jargon in the UI
- Risk categorisation logic must be explainable, not just a black box
