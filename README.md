# LexClear — Legal Contract Analyser

## Problem

4 out of 5 Indians sign contracts without reading them. 1 in 3 tenants face deposit disputes — all stemming from a gap between scanning and truly understanding legal documents.

## Target User

Anyone who works in a company or moves out of their hometown to live in a rented flat — people who regularly encounter legal contracts but lack the time or expertise to read them thoroughly.

## What We're Building

A Legal Contract Analyser with the following capabilities:

1. **Risk Categorisation** — Classifies every clause into High, Medium, or Low Risk so users know what to pay attention to.
2. **Chatbot** — Lets users drill down on key references and ask follow-up questions about specific clauses.
3. **Hidden Key References / Keywords** — Surfaces buried obligations, deadlines, and penalties that are easy to miss.
4. **Long Document Support** — Handles documents of 10 or more pages without loss of context.
5. **Multilingual** — Built for the Indian context, supporting regional languages alongside English.
